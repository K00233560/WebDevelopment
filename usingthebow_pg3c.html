About the Bow

via GIPHY

Rosin

A bow without rosin is like no bow at all. Without the rosin, the horsehairs just slide smoothly over the strings, creating almost no vibration.You can also see the rosin rising as the bow strikes the string in the clip above. If there was no rosin on the bow, it would not get the grip it needs to vibrate the strings
